mastergo-leela
===============
AHN, Nov 2019

Scripts to process the leela zero match game archive into a folder
of sgf files wivch can be imported into mastergo.

The leela zero match archive is here:

https://leela.online-go.com/zero/all_match.sgf.xz

To uncompress, use

$ unxz all_match.sgf.xz

The archive is from July 2019. I hope they update this at some point.

A copy of the archive is at

s3://ahn-uploads/all_match.sgf.xz

=== The End ===
